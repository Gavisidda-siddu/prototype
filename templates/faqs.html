Frequently Asked Questions, {{ user.name }}!

What types of heart-related services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive heart care services including diagnostics (ECG, ECHO, TMT), preventive cardiology, interventional procedures, and long-term heart health management.

How can I book an appointment with a cardiologist?

You can book an appointment online through our website, via phone, or by visiting our reception desk during working hours.

Are your cardiologists certified and experienced?

Yes, all our cardiologists are board-certified with years of experience in treating various cardiovascular conditions.

Do I need a referral to consult a heart specialist?

No referral is needed. You can directly book a consultation with our heart specialists.

What should I expect during my first heart consultation?

Your cardiologist will review your medical history, perform a physical exam, and may recommend tests like an ECG or blood work to assess your heart health.

Are diagnostic tests available in your clinic?

Yes, we offer on-site diagnostic facilities including ECG, Echocardiogram, TMT, Holter Monitoring, and blood tests.

Do you offer online or video consultations?

Yes, we provide online consultations for follow-ups, second opinions, and non-emergency cardiac concerns.

Is emergency heart care available at your facility?

Yes, our clinic is equipped to handle cardiac emergencies. Please call our emergency line for urgent assistance.

How do I prepare for a cardiac test or procedure?

Our team will give you specific instructions based on the test. Generally, fasting, avoiding caffeine, or stopping certain medications may be required.
